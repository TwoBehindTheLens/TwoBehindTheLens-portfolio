About Us

We're Julia and Yang, a passionate wildlife photographer couple who loves to travel across the world to photograph nature’s wildest moments.
Based in Sarawak, our work reflects our love for the untamed beauty of the wild. Through each image, we strive to be a voice for the voiceless, where most times photos speak what words can't.
Be part of the echo — follow our journey @twobehindthelens.
